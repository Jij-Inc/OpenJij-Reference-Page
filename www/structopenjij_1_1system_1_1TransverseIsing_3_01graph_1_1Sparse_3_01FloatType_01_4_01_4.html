Detailed Description

template<typename FloatType>
struct openjij::system::TransverseIsing< graph::Sparse< FloatType > >

naive Sparse TransverseIsing structure with discrete-time trotter spins (with Eigen implementation) The original Hamiltonian is like

\[ H(s) = s\left(\sum_{i<j}J_{ij} \sigma_i^z \sigma_j^z + \sum_{i}h_{i} \sigma_i^z \right) + \Gamma (1-s)\sum_i \sigma_i^x, \]

where \( \Gamma \) shows the strength of transverse field (referred to as gamma) and \( s \) shows the annealing schedule (referred to as s).

Quantum Monte Carlo simulation is performed under the finite (inverse) temperature ( \( \beta \) or beta).
